Ground Spreaders

Creepers also have weak stems, but unlike climbers, they spread horizontally along the ground. They often produce heavy fruits that would be difficult to carry if they grew upwards.

Key Characteristics: Fragile stems, horizontal growth, ground cover.

Examples: Pumpkin, Watermelon, and Strawberry.

🌱 Did you know?

Creepers are excellent for preventing soil erosion because they cover the ground like a natural carpet!
