Medium & Bushy

Shrubs are medium-sized woody plants taller than herbs but shorter than trees. Their height usually ranges from 6 to 10 meters. They have hard, thin stems that branch out near the base.

Key Characteristics: Hard stems, bushy appearance, branching near the ground.

Examples: Rose, Lemon, Hibiscus, and Jasmine.

🌱 Did you know?

Shrubs are often called "bushes" and are the most popular choice for creating natural privacy hedges in gardens.
