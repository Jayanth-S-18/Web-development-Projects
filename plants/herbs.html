Short & Delicate

Herbs are the smallest plants with soft, green, and tender stems. They usually complete their life cycle in one or two seasons. Unlike other plants, they do not have woody tissues.

Key Characteristics: Very short height, green stems, easily uprooted.

Usage: Mostly used for flavoring, medicine, and perfumes.

🌱 Did you know?

Banana plants are technically considered the world's largest herbs because they have no woody stem!
