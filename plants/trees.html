Tall & Strong

Trees are big, tall plants with a very thick, woody, and hard stem called a trunk. The trunk supports many branches that bear leaves, flowers, and fruits. They live for many years.

Key Characteristics: Thick bark, single main trunk, deep root systems.

Role: They act as the Earth's lungs by absorbing Carbon Dioxide.

🌱 Did you know?

The Bristlecone Pine is one of the oldest living trees, with some individuals living over 5,000 years!
